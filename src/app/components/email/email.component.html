Account & Data Deletion Requests

At Zency, we respect your privacy and your right to control your personal information.

Option 1: Delete Your Account Through the Zency App

Users can permanently delete their account directly from the Zency app using the Account Deletion feature. For security purposes, account deletion is verified through your registered mobile number and OTP authentication.

Option 2: Request Account Deletion via Email

Users can request their account deletion and data deletion by emailing us at: support@getzency.com

Please include your registered mobile number and any relevant account details to help us verify your identity. Zency will contact you in person to validate your identity before deletion is processed.

What Happens When Your Account Is Deleted?

Upon successful verification of your request:

  • Your Zency account will be permanently deleted.
  • Personal information associated with your account will be permanently removed from our active systems.
  • Order history, profile information, saved addresses, and other personal account data will be deleted unless retention is required by applicable laws or regulatory obligations.
  • Zency does not retain user data after deletion except where legally required for compliance, fraud prevention, taxation, or dispute resolution purposes.

Once the deletion process is completed, the account cannot be restored.

For any questions regarding account deletion or data privacy, please contact us at: support@getzency.com
