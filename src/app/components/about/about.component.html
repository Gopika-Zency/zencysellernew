About Us

Welcome to Zency formally “Witness Manera Private Limited”. At Zency we are redefining the way India shops for fashion, lifestyle and categories like jewellery, footwear, beauty, home essentials, electronics and much more. With our 15-minute ultrafast delivery, we bring trendy apparel, accessories, footwear, beauty, and lifestyle essentials straight to your doorstep because fashion should move as fast as you do. Zency takes advantage or technology and AI for its operational excellence and our team is committed to provide best service to all our customers and sellers.

How Zency Works

We have built a hyperlocal network of dark stores in India’s top metropolitan cities like Mumbai, Delhi NCR, Bangalore and Hyderabad powered by AI-driven logistics, predictive inventory stocking, and realtime order processing. This allows us to deliver premium fashion and beauty products in just 15 minutes, without compromising on variety, pricing, or quality. Shopping can be done on Zency App available on Android and iOS.

What Makes Zency Unique?

  • With Instant Fashion and Lifestyle at No Extra Cost, we ensure you get the latest fashion without paying extra for express delivery.
  • Curated from 2000+ Fashion D2C and Lifestyle Brands, we bring handpicked products from India’s booming direct-to-consumer (D2C) brands, ensuring uniqueness and quality.
  • Choose from More than 25000+ Designs and SKUs so that you get instantly what you want.
  • With AI-Optimized Pricing, our smart algorithms ensure fair pricing and optimal discounts, maximizing savings for both buyers and sellers.
  • With Exclusive Seller Partnerships Zency provides brands with a nationwide reach through its Witness Manera Channel, integrating their inventory with our platform for seamless local fulfilment.

Where We Operate

Zency is launching with 50+ dark stores across Mumbai, Delhi NCR, Bangalore, and Hyderabad in April 2025, expanding to Jaipur, Pune, Chennai, Kolkata, Ahmedabad, and Chandigarh by 2026.

Our Vision

Zency believes in empowering the nation by the use of cutting-edge technology and excellence in operations to improve the reach of D2C Brands and be fair to everyone who is involved with Zency from D2C entrepreneurs to our fast and efficient last mile delivery team. We believe that fast fashion should be delivered fast, should be accessible, and exciting as the world around us. Whether you need an office supply, a last-minute outfit for an event, a quick beauty fix, or stylish gifts on demand Zency delivers it in minutes. Zency also does not forget its responsibility towards environment sustainability.
